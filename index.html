Empowering Entrepreneurs in Botswana

Bizz Connectors is a digital platform that connects service providers, manufacturers, distributors, and customers in one trusted marketplace.

The Problem

Many small businesses and students struggle to advertise their products and services because traditional advertising platforms such as TV, radio, and newspapers are expensive. Online platforms also require paid promotions to reach large audiences.

Bizz Connectors provides an affordable digital solution where businesses can promote services, connect with customers, and grow their market reach.

Who We Serve

Service Providers

Hairdressers, barbers, tutors, mechanics, plumbers and freelancers can promote their services and receive bookings.

Customers

Customers can search for trusted service providers, compare reviews and book services easily.

Manufacturers

Manufacturers can promote products and connect with distributors and retailers.

Distributors

Distributors can expand their supply networks and partner with businesses across Botswana.
